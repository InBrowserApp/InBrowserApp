## Cong cu nay dung de lam gi

Dung cong cu nay de giai ma chuoi Base58 hoac tep van ban ve lai cac byte goc
ngay trong trinh duyet. No huu ich khi ban can kiem tra du lieu sao chep tu API,
vi, log, quy trinh QR hoac cac buoc nhap xuat ma khong muon gui noi dung len may
chu.

## Khi nao nen doi bang chu cai

Base58 khong chi co mot bang chu cai duy nhat. Bitcoin, Flickr va Ripple dung
thu tu ky tu khac nhau. Neu gia tri khong vuot qua kiem tra hoac giai ma ra ket
qua sai, hay doi sang bang chu cai ma he thong nguon dang su dung.

## Dieu can luu y

Khung ket qua se hien ban xem truoc UTF-8 khi cac byte da giai ma co the doc duoc
nhu van ban. Voi du lieu nhi phan hoac noi dung khong phai van ban, an toan hon
la tai tep .bin de xem byte goc. Khoang trang va xuong dong trong noi dung dan
vao se duoc bo qua, vi vay cac gia tri bi xuong dong trong email, tai lieu hoac
terminal van co the giai ma.
