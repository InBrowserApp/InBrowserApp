## What this tool does

This barcode generator creates CODE128, EAN, UPC, and other common barcode
formats directly in your browser. You can change the encoded text, bar size,
label position, and colors before exporting the result.

## Why it helps

Use it when you need a quick barcode for labels, inventory references,
packaging mockups, tickets, or internal documents. The preview updates
instantly, so it is easy to check spacing and readability before download.

## How it works

Choose a barcode format, enter the content, then tune width, height, margin,
colors, and label settings. When the preview looks right, download the barcode
as PNG, SVG, JPEG, or WebP.
