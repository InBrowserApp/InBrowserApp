## Ma Trang Thai HTTP la gi?

Ma trang thai HTTP la cac so co ba chu so duoc tra ve boi may chu de chi ket qua cua yeu cau cua may khach. Chung duoc nhom thanh nam loai:

- **1xx Thong tin:** Chi ra rang yeu cau da duoc nhan va qua trinh dang tiep tuc.
- **2xx Thanh cong:** Chi ra rang yeu cau da duoc nhan, hieu va chap nhan thanh cong.
- **3xx Chuyen huong:** Chi ra rang can thuc hien hanh dong bo sung de hoan thanh yeu cau.
- **4xx Loi May Khach:** Chi ra rang may khach da gap loi trong yeu cau (vi du, 404 Khong Tim Thay, 401 Chua Xac Thuc).
- **5xx Loi May Chu:** Chi ra rang may chu da gap loi khi xu ly yeu cau (vi du, 500 Loi May Chu Noi Bo, 503 Dich Vu Khong Kha Dung).
