## What is a business day?

A business day is any day your team, company, or client treats as a working day. For many schedules that means Monday through Friday without public holidays, but this calculator can also model regional calendars, custom weekends, or alternative workweeks.

## Business Day Rules

Start by choosing whether you want to define excluded weekend days or included working days directly. Then add optional holidays in YYYY-MM-DD format so the calculation matches your actual operating schedule.

- Select by: Weekend days / Working days
- Select which days are weekends.
- Select which days are working days.
- Holidays: Optional. Holidays on weekdays are excluded.
- Weekend holidays are already excluded by weekend settings.

## Count Business Days

Use this panel when you need the working-day total for a date range. The breakdown helps you verify the result by separating business days, total days, weekends, and holidays that fall on weekdays.

- Start date / End date
- Include start/end dates
- Business days
- Total days
- Weekend days
- Holidays (weekdays)

## Add/Subtract Business Days

Use this panel when you already know the base date and need a due date, follow-up date, or SLA target. It applies the same weekend and holiday rules as the range calculator, so both calculations stay aligned.

- Base date
- Business days
- Count start date as day 1
- Add / Subtract Date
