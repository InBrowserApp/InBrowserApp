## What is a business day?

A business day is a working day when businesses are typically open. It usually excludes weekends and public holidays, but you can customize the definition for your schedule.

## Business Day Rules

- Select by: Weekend days / Working days
- Select which days are weekends.
- Select which days are working days.
- Holidays: Optional. Holidays on weekdays are excluded.
- Weekend holidays are already excluded by weekend settings.

## Count Business Days

- Include start/end dates

- Weekend days
