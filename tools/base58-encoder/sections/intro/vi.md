## Vi sao Base58 duoc su dung

Base58 loai bo cac ky tu de nham lan nhu 0, O, I va l, vi vay gia tri da ma hoa de sao chep, doc va chia se hon trong URL, dia chi vi, payload QR va cac dinh danh can con nguoi kiem tra.

## Cong cu nay giup duoc gi

Cong cu nay chuyen van ban trong trinh duyet hoac tep tai len thanh Base58 voi bang chu cai Bitcoin, Flickr hoac Ripple. Ban co the doi bang chu cai truoc khi ma hoa, so sanh dau ra va xuat ket qua ma khong can gui du lieu len may chu.

## Khi nao nen dung

Hay dung khi ban can dang van ban gon hon cho du lieu nhi phan, muon thu xem mot dia chi hoac payload thay doi the nao giua cac bang chu cai Base58, hoac can chuan bi gia tri cho tai lieu, demo, kiem tra tuong thich va quy trinh ngoai tuyen.
