## What does this random number generator do?

This tool generates one or more random numbers inside a range you define. You
can work with integers or decimals, change the result count, use quick presets,
and open the latest draw in full screen.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your ranges, history, and exported
results stay on this device unless you choose to copy or download them.

## Practical tips

- Turn off duplicates when you need lottery-style or unique picks.
- When using decimals, set Min, Max, and Decimal Places together so the step
  size matches the range you want.
- Presets are shortcuts; you can still fine-tune Min, Max, Count, and duplicate
  rules afterward.
- Use history and the download action when you want to compare recent draws or
  keep a quick record.
