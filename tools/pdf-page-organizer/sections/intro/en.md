# PDF Page Organizer

PDF Page Organizer lets you reorder, rotate, and remove pages from a PDF without uploading the document to a server. It creates a new PDF from the page order you choose, leaving the original file unchanged.

## When to use it

Use it when you need to fix scanned documents, remove accidental blank pages, move attachments into the right sequence, or rotate pages that were captured sideways.

## How to organize pages

Upload a PDF, review the page list, then use the controls beside each page to move it up or down, rotate it clockwise, or remove it from the export. When the order looks right, generate and download the new PDF.

## Privacy and limits

Processing happens locally in your browser session. Password-protected or encrypted PDFs are not supported, and very large documents may take longer to read or export.
